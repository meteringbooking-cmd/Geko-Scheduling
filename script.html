📞 Meter Exchange Calling Flow
Customer outbound — smart meter booking
00:00
01
Opening & Introduction
Build rapport and confirm who you are speaking to.
"Hello, could I speak with [Customer Name] please?"

[If connected:]
"Good [morning/afternoon], [Customer Name], my name is [Your Name] and I'm calling from Geko on behalf of your energy supplier.

The reason for my call today is that we're contacting customers in your area because your property is eligible for a free smart meter upgrade — and I'd love to help get that booked in for you at a time that suits."
02
Customer Verification
Confirm identity before discussing account details.
"Before I go any further, I just need to run through a couple of quick security questions to confirm I'm speaking with the right person — is that okay?

Could you confirm your full name and the first line of your address, including your postcode?"
⚠ Do NOT read out account details until verified.
03
Eligibility & Discovery
Confirm meter setup and location.
"I can see on your account that you currently have a traditional meter fitted. We're arranging upgrades to a smart meter at no cost to you.

Just to confirm — can you tell me where your electricity and/or gas meter is located? (e.g., in a cupboard, outside wall, garage?)"
⚠ If they rent, they MUST confirm landlord permission before we book.
04
Pitching the Benefits
Explain the value proposition clearly.
"The upgrade is completely free. You'll get accurate bills (no more estimates) and a free in-home display to show you exactly what you're spending in real-time.

The whole installation takes around 1.5 to 2.5 hours. There'll be a short power interruption of around 5 minutes, so we just ask you to save any computer work beforehand. Sound good?"
05
Secure Appointment
Fill in the booking details below.
"Brilliant. What dates work best for you? We have morning slots (8am-1pm) and afternoon slots (1pm-6pm)."
06
Wrap-Up & Confirmation
Lock in the details and end the call professionally.
"Perfect, I've booked that in. Just to confirm: your appointment is on [Date] for the [Morning/Afternoon].

You'll receive a confirmation text/email shortly. If you need to rearrange, please let us know 48 hours in advance.

Please ensure an adult is home, the meter area is clear, and computers are saved. Thank you for your time, have a lovely day!"
📞
Schedule a Callback
Customer is busy or needs landlord permission.
"No problem at all — I completely understand. When would be the best time for me to call you back?"

❓ Objection Handling
1. "How do I know you're genuine?"▾
"I'm not asking for payment details or passwords. I just need to confirm your address to ensure I'm speaking to the account holder. You can always call us back via the number on your bill."
2. "I heard they catch fire/hack you."▾
"Smart meters use very low-level radio signals, similar to a baby monitor, meeting all strict UK safety standards. Your data is end-to-end encrypted and regulated by Ofgem."
3. "I'm not in / Can someone else be there?"▾
"Yes! As long as the person is over 18 and you give them permission, that's perfectly fine."
4. "I already have one."▾
"Apologies, our records must be outdated. I'll note this down so you aren't contacted again."
