NearBy Filter

When NearBy is enabled and you click on a job card, the engineer list is filtered to show only engineers who have at least one job scheduled within 40 minutes drive of the selected job's postcode.

This helps you quickly identify which engineers are already working in the same area and could be reassigned or given additional nearby jobs with minimal travel.

Click any job card while NearBy is on to apply the filter
